Data Collection Disclosure - CiFlow

Last updated: July 13, 2025

Data Collection Statement

✅ CiFlow does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data to external servers.

What This Means

✅ Local Storage Only

  • All your business data (inventory, sales, receipts) is stored locally on your device
  • No personal information is sent to our servers
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No cloud storage of your business data

✅ No Personal Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Names, addresses, or contact information
  • ❌ Business financial data
  • ❌ Customer information
  • ❌ Payment details
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Photos or files you create in the app
  • ❌ Personal preferences or settings

✅ Third-Party Services

The only data collection comes from:

Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Collects advertising identifiers for ad personalization
  • This is handled entirely by Google, not by us
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • No personal business data is shared with AdMob

Your Data, Your Control

Complete Ownership

  • ✓ You own 100% of your business data
  • ✓ Data stays on your device at all times
  • ✓ Uninstalling the app removes all data
  • ✓ No data recovery needed from us

No Data Requests

  • ✓ We cannot access your business information
  • ✓ We cannot provide data exports (you control your own exports)
  • ✓ We cannot delete your data remotely (you control deletion)
  • ✓ We have no servers storing your information

Technical Details

App Permissions

  • Camera: Only for barcode scanning (processed locally)
  • Storage: Only for saving files on your device
  • Internet: Only for displaying ads, not for data transmission

Data Flow

Your Device ← → Your Data (Local Storage)
Your Device ← → Google AdMob (Ads Only)
Your Device ✗ Our Servers (NO CONNECTION)
