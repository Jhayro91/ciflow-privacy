Privacy Policy for CiFlow

Last updated: July 13, 2025

Introduction

CiFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Point of Sale (POS) mobile application ("CiFlow" or "the App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

1. Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Business Information: Business name, address, phone number, and email address
  • Product Data: Product names, prices, descriptions, and inventory information
  • Sales Data: Transaction records, sales history, and receipt information
  • Configuration Data: App settings, preferences, and customization options

2. Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device:

  • Device Identifiers: Device ID, advertising ID, and other unique identifiers
  • Technical Information: Operating system version, app version, device model
  • Usage Information: App usage patterns, feature usage, and crash reports

3. Camera and Storage Permissions

The App requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Camera Access: For barcode scanning functionality
  • Storage Access: For data backup, export/import features, and receipt generation
  • File Access: For managing product images and business documents

How We Use Your Information

1. Core App Functionality

  • Manage your inventory and product catalog
  • Process sales transactions and generate receipts
  • Maintain sales history and analytics
  • Provide barcode scanning capabilities
  • Enable data backup and synchronization

2. App Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve app performance
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features and enhancements
  • Provide customer support

3. Advertising

  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
  • Personalize ad content based on app usage (with your consent)
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information to provide personalized ads. The information collected by AdMob includes:

  • Advertising ID: Used for personalized advertising and ad measurement
  • Device Information: IP address, user agent, device identifiers
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (if permitted)
  • Usage Data: Interaction with ads and app usage patterns

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Analytics Services

We may use analytics services to understand how users interact with our App. These services may collect:

  • App usage statistics
  • Feature usage patterns
  • Performance metrics
  • Crash reports and error logs

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device, including:

  • Business information and settings
  • Product inventory and descriptions
  • Sales transactions and history
  • Generated receipts and reports

Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Data Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard methods
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Security patches and updates are provided regularly
  • Secure Transmission: Data transmission uses secure protocols when applicable

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Stored on your device until you delete the app or clear data
  • Backup Data: Retained according to your backup settings and preferences
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained for app improvement

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

You have full control over your data through the app interface:

  • Access: View and manage all your data within the app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Export: Export your data using the built-in backup functionality
  • Correction: Modify or update your information directly through the app
Note: Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information without needing to contact us.

Advertising Preferences

You can control advertising preferences:

  • Opt-out: Disable personalized ads through your device's advertising settings
  • Ad Choices: Use the AdChoices icon in ads to manage preferences
  • Reset Advertising ID: Reset your advertising identifier in device settings

Permissions Management

You can manage app permissions through your device settings:

  • Camera: Required for barcode scanning (can be disabled)
  • Storage: Required for data management (essential for app functionality)
  • Network: Required for ads and optional cloud features

Children's Privacy

CiFlow is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Parental Responsibility: If you are a parent or guardian, you are responsible for monitoring your child's use of mobile applications and ensuring they do not provide personal information without your consent. Parents and guardians should review this Privacy Policy and discuss appropriate app usage with their children.

If you become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information through the app, you should immediately delete the app and clear any stored data from the device.

International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the country where our servers are located, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries with different privacy laws. By using the App, you consent to such transfers.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing in-app notifications for significant changes

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting our systems:

  • We will assess the impact and notify users if necessary
  • Since most data is stored locally on your device, you maintain primary control over your information security
  • We recommend regularly backing up your data and keeping your device secure with passwords/biometric locks
